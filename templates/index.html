Everything you need to tend your grove

Aspen brings structure and intention to the people side of your work — without the spreadsheets, the sticky notes, or the sense that you are being watched.

  • 01

    Rooted on your device

    Honest notes need a private home. Everything you write stays local — never sold, mined, or shared. Nothing leaves your device without your word.

  • 02

    Four quiet groves

    Sort the people who matter into Align, Guide, Partner, and Network — so you always know who advocates for you, who you are growing, and who keeps you connected.

  • 03

    Arrive prepared

    Before each conversation, Aspen gathers what happened last time, what you promised, and what to ask — so you begin where you left off, not from nothing.

  • 04

    Keep your word

    Track the commitments you make and the ones made to you. Trust is built on follow-through, and Aspen makes sure nothing quietly slips.

  • 05

    Hold the moments

    Keep the hallway exchange, the hard feedback, the win you shared — captured in a line or two while it is still warm.

  • 06

    Where your work already is

    A composed desktop app for deep sessions and a CLI for the terminal. The same relationships, the same privacy, wherever you already work.

From scattered to rooted, in three unhurried steps

    Grow

    Step back, notice the patterns, and watch small, consistent acts of care compound over time.
